Business Analysis

10 Key Business Insights

Data-driven findings from the full EDA — ready for business decision-making.

INSIGHT #01
🏆 Top Revenue Category
Baby Care dominates with the highest revenue share across all 10 categories — driven by high-ticket Diapers and Baby Food SKUs with strong repeat purchase behaviour.
₹6.6L — 18.4% share
INSIGHT #02
🏙️ Tier-2 City Surprise
Surat ranks #1 in total revenue among all 15 cities — outperforming metro cities like Chennai and Kolkata. A clear signal of under-served demand in Tier-2 India.
Surat leads with ₹2.5L+
INSIGHT #03
📅 Festive Seasonality
October is consistently the peak month across both 2023 and 2024. Q4 (Oct–Dec) outperforms Q1 by 30–40%, driven by Diwali, Dussehra, and year-end demand.
Q4 vs Q1: +35% revenue
INSIGHT #04
💸 Discount Sweet Spot
Moderate discounts of 6–10% drive the highest order volume AND maintain strong avg revenue per order. Heavy discounts (16%+) actually reduce avg revenue by ₹70+ per order.
6–10% = optimal band
INSIGHT #05
💳 UPI Dominance
UPI accounts for 40% of all orders — India's digital payment revolution is clearly reflected. Blinkit Wallet at only 10% represents a significant growth opportunity with cashback incentives.
UPI: 40% | Wallet: 10%
INSIGHT #06
👥 Regular Customers Rule
Regular customers contribute 50.7% of all orders, making them the backbone of Blinkit's revenue. Converting New → Regular customers is the single biggest growth lever available.
Regular: 50.7% of orders
INSIGHT #07
🚚 Speed ≠ Satisfaction
Delivery time has a near-zero correlation with customer ratings (Pearson r = −0.002). Product quality, freshness, and order accuracy are what truly drive satisfaction.
Pearson r = −0.002
INSIGHT #08
📆 Weekday vs Weekend
Weekday orders are 2.5× weekend orders, with ₹19 higher average order value — suggesting professional/office demographics drive most of Blinkit's revenue.
Weekday AOV: ₹345 vs ₹326
INSIGHT #09
👑 Premium Discount Paradox
Despite receiving 3× higher average discounts than New customers, Premium customers don't generate proportionally more revenue — suggesting margin erosion without equivalent loyalty return.
Premium avg disc: 15%
INSIGHT #10
📈 YoY Growth
Consistent year-over-year growth with 2024 outperforming 2023 in most months. Revenue trajectory shows acceleration in festive quarters with stable off-season performance.
~8–12% YoY growth
