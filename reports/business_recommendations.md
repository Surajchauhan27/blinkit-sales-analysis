## 4. Discount Strategy Insights

### 4.1 Current Discount Pattern
| Discount Band | Order Count | Avg Revenue/Order |
|--------------|-------------|-------------------|
| No Discount | ~3,100 | ₹315 |
| 1–5% | ~2,200 | ₹330 |
| 6–10% | ~2,400 | ₹345 |
| 11–15% | ~1,600 | ₹360 |
| 16%+ | ~1,200 | ₹290 |

### 4.2 Key Finding
- **Moderate discounts (6–10%) maximise both volume AND revenue per order**
- **Heavy discounts (16%+) actually reduce avg revenue per order by ~₹55–70**
- Premium customers receive 3× higher average discounts — ROI must be validated

**Recommendation:**
- Cap discount ceiling at 15% for Premium customers — beyond this, margin erosion outweighs loyalty benefit
- Shift "16%+ discount" budget to loyalty rewards (cashback, Blinkit Wallet credits)
- Run A/B tests: compare 8% discount vs ₹30 cashback on identical orders

### 5.2 Key Finding
- **Regular customers are the backbone** — 50%+ of orders and revenue
- **Premium customers** get heavy discounts but don't proportionally drive more revenue
- **New customer conversion to Regular** is the biggest growth lever

**Recommendation:**
- Launch "Blinkit Plus" loyalty program: 3 orders → convert New to Regular
- Reduce Premium discount from 12% → 8% and replace with exclusive features (priority delivery, early access to deals)
- Implement referral program: Regular customers refer friends → both get ₹50 wallet credit

## 7. Delivery Operations

### 7.1 Key Finding
- **Delivery time has negligible correlation with ratings** (Pearson r ≈ −0.002)
- Customer satisfaction is driven by **product quality and accuracy**, not speed
- Metro cities average 18 min; Tier-2 cities average 32 min

**Recommendation:**
- Focus delivery investment on **product packaging quality and freshness** (higher ROI than speed improvement)
- For Tier-2 cities: target <25 min delivery for high-frequency categories (Dairy, Bread)
- Add "Delivery scheduled in X minutes" real-time notifications — improves perceived speed

## 8. Festive & Seasonal Strategy

### 8.1 Revenue Pattern
- **Q4 (Oct–Dec) outperforms Q1 by 30–40%** — Diwali/festive season impact
- **October is the peak month** consistently across both years
- **February is the weakest month** — ideal for targeted promotions
- YoY growth of ~8–12% between 2023 and 2024

**Recommendation:**
- Pre-stock high-demand categories (Beverages, Frozen Foods, Snacks) from late September
- Run "Blinkit Diwali Hamper" bundles — pre-order in October for festive gifting
- Launch "February Fest" campaign with 3-day flash sales to offset the seasonal dip
- Invest in supply chain capacity expansion for Q4 — current infrastructure strained during festive peaks

## 11. Conclusion

Blinkit's sales data reveals a healthy, growing business with clear opportunities:

1. **Baby Care and Staples are revenue pillars** — protect and grow with subscriptions and bundles
2. **Moderate discounts (6–10%) are the sweet spot** — avoid heavy discounting
3. **Tier-2 cities are underserved gems** — Surat and Chandigarh show demand exceeding supply
4. **Festive seasonality is strong** — pre-stock and pre-market Q4 heavily
5. **Digital payments are winning** — accelerate Blinkit Wallet adoption with cashback

By aligning product strategy, discount policy, and city-level operations with these data-driven insights, Blinkit can improve profitability, customer retention, and market penetration across India.
